Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

At GoalForge, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy document explains how our mobile application handles user data. Our application is designed to be a clean, offline-first sport planning utility, collecting the absolute minimum amount of data required to deliver standard advertising.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

GoalForge is fully functional without requiring any registration or account creation. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII), such as:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or social media accounts.
  • Your precise location (GPS data) or background tracking information.
  • Health, medical, physiological data, or physical metrics.

2. Integrated Advertising Services

To keep GoalForge 100% free with no in-app purchases or paywalls, we deliver advertisements using the official Unity Ads SDK. To serve these ads, the SDK may process:

  • Device Advertising Identifier: IDFA (iOS) / GAID (Android) to deliver contextual and non-personalized ads, strictly in compliance with operating system privacy settings.
  • Basic Performance Metrics: Information regarding ad impressions, clicks, and video completions to measure campaign performance.

Compliance Note: This application does not integrate or use Firebase Analytics, AppsFlyer, or any other user-attribution trackers in this version.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

4. Data Storage and Security

All your training plans, personal goals, and drill notes are stored locally on your physical device. We do not utilize external cloud databases to transfer your plans, which completely prevents remote data breaches or unauthorized access.
