Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how data is handled within the Goal-Forge mobile application, developed by George Whitaker.

1. Core App Data & Processing

Goal-Forge is built to provide workout organization, customized training routines, plans, metrics, and progress logs. Most data generated by your activities remains stored locally on your device to ensure quick access and minimal latency.

2. Camera and Photo Library Access

The app may request permission to access your device’s camera or photo gallery. This access is strictly optional and is utilized exclusively when you choose to upload images to log your training progression or customize your profile. No images are transferred to our servers without your explicit action.

3. Performance Analytics Partners

To identify optimization errors, maintain stability, and continuously enhance the application interface, we integrate analytics tools provided by Google (Firebase). These services process technical application logs and crash reports to help us diagnose functionality failures.

4. Marketing and Attribution

We work with AppsFlyer, an industry-standard attribution platform, to understand marketing performance and analyze patterns regarding how users discover our application. This data helps us optimize deployment strategies and marketing allocation.

5. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, the application may display a request to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. You have the full right to decline this request. Declining tracking will not limit or restrict the core features, performance, or availability of Goal-Forge.

6. Web Content Display (WebView)

The application may employ WebView technologies to display specific external web-based text resources, legal disclosures, or documents (including this Privacy Policy page) directly inside the interface for a smoother experience.

7. Health & Medical Disclaimer

Goal-Forge is a tool intended for general fitness tracking and training plan organization. It does not provide medical guidance, professional diagnoses, or clinical therapy. The service does not replace standard medical advice or consultation with health professionals.
