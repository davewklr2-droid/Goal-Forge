About Goal-Forge

My name is George Whitaker. As an independent mobile software engineer, I strive to design applications that bring measurable utility, structure, and technological simplicity into sport analytics and execution.

Goal-Forge is a comprehensive environment for planning out complex football drills, scheduling routines, and monitoring specific athletic workloads over cycles. It is tailored to streamline information tracking for players who value structure.

Current Monetization Status:
Goal-Forge currently features no paid unlocks, locked features, or recurrent subscription systems. All features are completely open and accessible to our initial users.

In upcoming product milestones, I intend to safely build and roll out premium subscription layers or advanced customization tiers. This future evolution will help support development, sustain server stability, and allow me to introduce deep, data-driven optimization mechanics into the system.
